Privacy Policy – Obsesive App

Last updated: December 18, 2025

Obsesive is a fitness and workout planning application based on scientific evidence. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when using the Obsesive app available on Google Play.

1. General Information

App name: Obsesive
Developer: Obsesive Labs
Contact email: obsesiveapp@gmail.com

2. Use of the App Without an Account

Obsesive can be used without creating an account. Users can access all core features of the app, including creating routines, tracking workout history, analyzing training statistics, and browsing exercises.

When used without an account, all data is stored locally on the device. If the device is lost or changed, this data cannot be recovered.

3. Information We Collect

Personal information (optional)

  • Email address (only if the user creates an account)

User-generated content

  • Workout routines
  • Workout and exercise history
  • Training statistics and muscle group analysis

App usage and performance data

  • App interactions
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Other app performance data

Technical identifiers

  • Device or other identifiers used for app functionality, analytics, and advertising

4. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Precise location data
  • Web browsing history
  • Contacts or messages between users
  • List of installed applications
  • Clinical or medical health data

Obsesive is not classified as a regulated medical application.

5. How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Analyze workouts and training statistics
  • Manage user accounts (when applicable)
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Display advertisements

6. Data Sharing

Obsesive does not sell personal data. Limited technical data may be shared with trusted third-party services strictly for app operation.

  • Firebase (Google): authentication, analytics, crash reporting
  • Google AdMob: advertising services

7. Advertising

Ads are provided through Google AdMob. Ads do not block core features, and no payment is required to use the app. Email addresses are not used for ad personalization.

8. Data Security

  • Data is transmitted using secure connections (HTTPS / TLS)
  • Industry-standard Google services are used

9. Account and Data Deletion

Users who create an account can delete their account and associated data directly from within the app.

Public account deletion guide:
https://alancontreh.github.io/obsesive-delete-account/

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page.

This Privacy Policy applies to the Obsesive app available on Google Play.
