Privacy Policy – Obsesive

Last updated: December 2025

```

1. General Information

App name: Obsesive
Developer: Obsesive Labs
Platform: Android (Google Play)

Obsesive is a gym and workout planning application based on scientific evidence. The app can be used with or without creating an account. Creating an account is completely optional.

2. Using the App Without an Account

Users can access all core features of the app without signing in, including:

  • Creating and saving workout routines
  • Recording workout history
  • Analyzing training volume and routines
  • Viewing statistics per muscle group
  • Using the workout timer
  • Browsing exercises and scientific articles

When the app is used without an account, all data is stored locally on the device only. If the user changes or loses their device, this information will be permanently lost and cannot be recovered.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information that may be collected

a) Personal information (optional)

  • Email address (only if the user creates an account)

b) User-generated content

  • Workout routines
  • Workout and exercise history
  • Training statistics and analysis per muscle group
  • App-related configurations

c) App usage and performance data

  • App interactions
  • Crash logs
  • Diagnostics and performance data

d) Technical identifiers

  • Device identifiers or other technical identifiers provided by third-party services (such as Firebase or AdMob)

4. Information We Do NOT Collect

Obsesive does not collect:

  • Precise location data
  • Web browsing history
  • Contacts
  • Messages between users
  • List of installed apps
  • Clinical or medical health data

Obsesive is not classified as a regulated medical application.

5. How We Use the Information

Collected data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Manage user accounts (when an account exists)
  • Synchronize data across devices for logged-in users
  • Analyze app usage and improve performance
  • Detect, diagnose, and fix bugs or crashes
  • Display advertisements within the app

6. Data Sharing with Third Parties

Obsesive does not sell personal data.

Certain technical data may be shared with trusted third-party services strictly for app operation:

  • Firebase (Google): authentication, database, analytics, crash reporting
  • Google AdMob: displaying advertisements

These services may process device or user identifiers and aggregated or pseudonymized usage data. The user's email address is not used for ad targeting.

7. Advertising

Obsesive displays ads through Google AdMob:

  • Ads do not block core features
  • No payment is required to access functionality
  • Email addresses are not used for direct ad personalization

8. Data Security

  • All data is transmitted over secure connections (HTTPS / TLS)
  • Industry-standard Google services are used
  • Access to data is limited to what is strictly necessary

9. Account and Data Deletion

Users who create an account can delete their account and associated personal data directly from within the app.

The account deletion process:

  • Deletes the user account
  • Removes associated personal data
  • May require re-authentication for security reasons

Public account deletion guide:
https://alancontreh.github.io/obsesive-delete-account/

Workout routines shared via codes are not deleted, as they become independent content once shared.

10. User Rights

  • Use the app without providing personal information
  • Access, modify, or delete their account
  • Stop using the app at any time

11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
```
